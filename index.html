What is Project StarSeed?

Project StarSeed is the current codename for a game I've been thinking of developing. It's an expansion of my first C# programming project, Starbattle (Pictured Below) and is heavily influenced by Neveron, a currently defunct game that has been offline since 2014.

The game will be a long-term (like months long) multiplayer empire-building game, with a hex-grid tile based combat system, research, industry, and ship design components. It will include both space and ground combat, and the empire management will start at a intra-planetary scale, and with technology evolve into interplanetary and interstellar.

As this is an extremely ambitious idea (especially given my lack of motivation to work on it because I don't personally know anyone who would actually be interested in it), it will be developed in stages, starting at the intra-planetary scale with no space travel, and possibly evolving to have the interplanetary and interstellar scales. This is why it's named Project Starseed: The intra-planetary scale game will be the seed that grows into an interstellar one as I develop the systems, and as the player factions research and develop space travel.
